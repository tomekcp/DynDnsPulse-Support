## Privacy Policy
**Last Updated: March 2026**

**1. No Data Collection**
DynDnsPulse is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any personal user data. There are no analytics, trackers, or third-party SDKs (other than the standard Cloudflare API) included in the app.

**2. Local Storage**
All sensitive information, such as your Cloudflare API Token and Zone ID, is stored securely on your own Mac using the macOS Keychain or encrypted UserDefaults. This data never leaves your device except for the direct communication with Cloudflare.

**3. Direct Communication**
To provide its service, the app connects to:
* **ipify.org** (or similar services) to retrieve your public IP address.
* **Cloudflare API** to update your DNS records.

Please refer to the privacy policies of these providers for information on how they handle standard connection logs.
